# Douchbag_Game-
In here, we will use python classes to copy the game "Duchbag Gym" using classes of python

Needs:
        Energy: If Energy goes below 0%, The player faints and the game restarts from zero
        Health: If It goes down than 30% player starts loosing gains by 2% per move or workout.
        Fat: If it goes over 30%, player gains reduces by 50% than normal
        
        Status


In this project we will be using the following classes:

Player

if the player works out upper body:
    health stays the same
    fat goes down 3%
    energy reduces by 30%
    Upper body strength increases 5%

if the player works out lower body:
    health stays the same
    fat goes down 3%
    energy reduces by 40%
    Lower body strength increases 5%

if player does cardio:
    health up 5%
    energy reduces 50%
    fat down 10%

if player eats a Healthy meal:
    health goes up 15%
    fat goes up 15%
    energy goes up 20%

if player eats junk meal:
    health goes down 5%
    fat goes up 50%
    energy goes up 90%

supplements:

protein: 
    health increases 10
    energy increase 10
    pump multiplies by 1.2

creatine:
    health increases 10
    energy increase 10
    pump multiplies by 2

esteroid:
    health decreases 30
    energy increase 20
    pump multiplies by 5
